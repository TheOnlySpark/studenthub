Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the minimum amount of attendance required by VISTAS for exam entry

    The minimum percentage of attendance required is 75%. In the cases of medical exemptions this percentage may drop around 65%.

  • How can I apply for Medical exemption if my attendance is lower than 75%

    Present a copy of a medical certificate given by the doctor to your class incharge faculty. Follow further instructions given by them. Usually you would be expected to write a letter and get signatures from both your class incharge and HOD. This letter should then be submitted to your class incharge with the medical certificate.

  • How are the marks of exams calculated and consolidated at the end of each semester

    2 CAT exams (50 marks each which are calculated for 10 marks total) and 1 model session (calculated for 20 marks) account for 40% ; These are also reflected as internal marks. Semester end exams which are tested for 100 marks make up to 60% of the final CGPA. [(2 CATs + 1 Model)+(Sem end) = 40 + 60].

  • If I am facing a problem with a faculty or a student where can I raise a complaint

    If the complaint is within the class please approach the class incharge faculty. If the complain is regarding any faculty or facility, approach your student class representative and raise a issue/complaint in the department or your respective HOD.

  • What is the anti-ragging scheme?

    VISTAS is a Anti-Ragging instituition. If you face anything related to ragging you can file for compliance with the assistance of your class incharge faculty. No matter how minor, We are here for you. Help is here. SAY NO TO BULLYING!
